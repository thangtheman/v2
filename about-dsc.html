About DSC

Dr. Subhash Chandra


The name - Dr. Subhash Chandra (DSC), can be simply summarized in three simple, words – Visionary, Philanthropist and a Leader.

A leader, who has proven his potential to nurture the nation, and to elevate it to a global stage. A leader, who has the power to fight against all odds, and to solve the challenges faced by the nation. A leader, who has immensely contributed to India’s liberalization story in the early 90s, by gifting the nation, not just its first private satellite television channel, but an entire industry in itself, creating millions of jobs.

DSC’s wisdom, determination, and vision has immense potential to empower every Indian, in the pursuit of bringing in a paradigm shift in the perception levels of our country; and a much required change across the nation.

DSC, a self-made man, has consistently demonstrated his ability to identify new businesses and lead them on the path of success. He shares his rich experience on the DSCShow, which is watched by over 230 Mn viewers across the nation. With this popular show, he is now a guiding force for the youth of the nation, enriching and empowering their lives. 

For his contributions to the industry, DSC has been awarded the International Emmy Directorate Award at their 39th International Emmy Awards night in New York and has also been Honored with the Doctorate of Business Administration by the University of East London.

DSC’s philanthropic activities, under the brand umbrella – DSC Foundation, have contributed immensely to the betterment of the society. He has set up TALEEM (Transnational Alternate Learning for Emancipation and Empowerment through Multimedia) to provide access to quality education through distance and open learning. He is also the Chairman of the Ekal Vidyalaya Foundation of India — a movement to eradicate illiteracy from rural and tribal India. The Foundation provides free education to millions of tribal children across thousands of villages in India, through one-teacher schools. He is the moving force behind the Global Vipassana Foundation — a trust set up to help people raise their spiritual quotient. DSC is also the Founder Chairman of Global Foundation for Civilization Harmony (India) (GFCH), an Eastern initiative for conflict avoidance and a partner of the United Nations Alliance of Civilization (UNAOC). The founder patrons of GFCH include His Holiness Dalai Lama and former President of India Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, among others.

DSC’s vision is to take India to a global stage and to eradicate the challenges faced by the nation at a domestic and at an international level. A global leader who takes immense pride in his roots and his country, a firm a believer of “Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam” (which means One World – One Family), and an author of the bestseller “The Z Factor”; DSC is working towards helping India to face and eradicate the challenges; and to create a better tomorrow.

DSC’s Message to the Nation


India has immense potential to rise up as a global super power and the youth of our nation is the driving force to achieve the same. I take immense pride in being an Indian first, and I envision our nation to thrive up, against all odds and challenges. As a citizen of the nation, it is my duty and responsibility to work towards eradicating the challenges faced by my fellow citizens; which are restricting the growth towards rising up and shining bright on the global stage.

I have always advised the youth of our nation, that their productivity and chances of achieving success, substantially increase, if Honesty is the epicenter of one’s efforts. So with complete Honesty and Integrity, I take up the responsibility to prepare today’s youth for a better tomorrow. And I firmly believe that the time is now. It is time for the non productive elements like corruption and fraudulence to be wiped off completely from the nation. It is time to redefine the word politics for the nation. It is time to make public service the focal point of our attention. And for this to happen, we all must start NOW.

It pains me a lot to see the opportunities being missed by young and potential citizens of our nation, purely due to lack of confidence and determination. It pains me to see that there are still such heavy number of innocent lives, which are deprived of basic amenities. Young and potential minds across the nation, especially in the rural areas are not being optimally utilized to uplift our nation. There is a strong lack of confidence, determination and most above a burning desire to contribute towards your nation. It is utmost critical for the nation to come together and fight against these hindrances. One needs to create awareness amongst the youth to discover and aptly estimate their potential. If one has a strong determination and belief in oneself first, and then in one’s dreams; there is nothing in this world which can stop you.

As a responsible citizen of the nation first, and then as a leader; I have taken up the owness, to bring about this level of awareness and change amongst the society, especially the youth. For I firmly believe that my experience, expertise and knowledge gained, empowers me to deliver. Having established what I have till date, I am now giving substantial time towards the socio-economic well being of the society. But I surely cannot do this alone, and for which I need your support and most above, your indulgence and participation. With this message, I appeal to the youth of our nation to rise up and be aware of your rights and responsibilities. With the advent of technology, the digital mediums like this website or the social media platforms, enable us to exchange thoughts and ideas, in making this nation a global super power. Let us together build this nation forward. I espouse the belief of “Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam” which means the entire World is One Family. And hence I firmly believe that we all being a part of One Family, must work towards betterment of the nation, unitedly. Our Family needs us. Our nation needs us.

Let us begin by identifying those problems across the nation, and by working towards finding solutions to eradicate them. Please write to me on onenation@dsc.com and lets start this journey of a Better India!

Yours truly,
