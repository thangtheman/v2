Ekal Vidyalaya Foundation

The overriding philosophy is to take a holistic approach to social and economic development. The Ekal movement is the largest grassroots, non-government education movement operating in remote and tribal villages of India. DSC is Ekal Global chairman.

Taleem Research Foundation

TALEEM Research Foundation was set up in 1996 as an autonomous, literary, scientific and charitable society and trust. Social science research, capacity building and alternate learning initiatives are its three main areas of interest. DSC is on the governing board.
