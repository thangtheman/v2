Recieving the CIF Chanchlani Global Indian Award 2016

Dr. Subhash Chandra, Chairman – Essel Group & ZEE to receive the prestigious Canada India Foundation (CIF) Chanchlani Global Indian Award

It is always a matter of immense pride and pleasure to share the moments of joy. Today, I want to share with you some good news for our Zee Family. I was privileged to receive the CIF Chanchlani Global Indian Award 2016. The award is result of the perseverance and hard work of our ZEE family around the world. I am extremely blessed to have such a dedicated team. When I was informed about this award, I was exhilarated for two reasons than one. One; of-course for being nominated to receive such a prestigious award; and Two; for being able to represent my country at this esteemed forum. The word “Indian” written on this trophy, makes me proud and happy than anything else.

People keep asking me the secret behind my success value; and my only response to them has been, that the teachings which I have received from my grandfather, are my biggest and most precious assets. They keep me focused on the path of truth; determined to achieve the set goals; and most above, they imbibe the “never give up” approach within me.

The award reception also gave me an opportunity to meet interesting personalities such as Hon. Kathleen Wynee, Premier of Ontario province & Hon. Navdeep Bains, Minister of Innovation, Science and Economic Development. I had the opportunity to have some fruitful disucssions with them and hope that those would help our Zee family and Indo-Canadian community to come forward and support our work in Ekal Vidyalaya in Canada.

Along with receiving the award, I felt extremely happy to provide the award money of $50,000 to Ekal Vidyalaya in Canada. I also had the chance to visit & meet some of the hardworking volunteers of Ekal Canada and spend some quality time with them.

Ekal Vidyalaya, I believe is an initiative which is capable of changing the face of our country. It is bringing pride back to the people of rural India by making them self sufficient. According to me, Ekal is helping to create a solid foundation for rural India to grow and rise on its feet.

I always say this, if you get a chance, do visit one of our Ekal Vidyalayas in rural India and you will understand the difference and the harmonious atmosphere prevelant there.

I once again would like to thank the Canada India Foundation for honoring me with the award. This truly means a lot to me. I look forward to interacting with you all more with my blogs going forward.
